About Aurum

“Aurum” means “golden” in Latin. We want to pay tribute to one of the best time in a day to take pictures. “Golden” is also about the memories that we take through our lenses: Unforgettable, precious memories.

We are a group of passionate photographers that can capture the “golden” moments of your life.
